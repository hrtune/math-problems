Prove
\[ z \in \overline{A \cap B} = z \in \overline{A} \cup \overline{B} \]
From the right side,
\[ z \in \overline{A} \cup \overline{B} \]
iff
\[ (z\in \overline{A})\ OR\ (z \in \overline{B}) \]
iff
\[ \overline{(z \in A)\ AND \ (z \in B)} \]

iff
\[ z \in \overline{A \cap B} \]
